Wooden Boxes

Our Wooden Boxes are versatile, custom-built packaging solutions crafted from a combination of pinewood and plywood panels. Designed for safe transport of industrial components, machinery parts, retail goods, and household items, these boxes feature solid nail or screw assembled construction that withstands rough handling during transit and storage. Suitable for one-way shipments or repeated use, they can be built to any size or shape to match your product requirements, with optional heat treatment for export consignments.
