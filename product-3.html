Pinewood Two-Way Pallet

Our Pinewood Two-Way Pallets are crafted from quality pine wood stringers and deck boards — the industry standard for domestic and export logistics. The natural pine construction offers an ideal strength-to-weight ratio. Stringers run the full pallet length with forklift entry from two sides. Heat treatment (ISPM-15) is available, making these pallets suitable for international export to USA, EU, Australia, and beyond.
