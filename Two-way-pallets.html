Two way pallets

Our Two Way Pallets are constructed from premium-grade plywood decks mounted on hardwood stringers, allowing forklift and pallet jack entry from two opposite sides. The smooth plywood surface prevents product damage and provides uniform load distribution. These pallets are lightweight yet strong, making them a cost-effective choice for FMCG, pharmaceutical, and light industrial applications. Available in standard and custom sizes.
