Pinewood Four-Way Pallet

Our Pinewood Four-Way Pallets provide the versatility of forklift entry from all four sides using either notched stringer construction or a full block base. Built from quality pine, these pallets are lightweight, durable, and ideal for high-throughput distribution centres, rack storage systems, and international export. ISPM-15 heat treatment is available as standard for all export-bound pallets.
