Bubble Polythene (Bubble Wrap)

Our Bubble Polythene (Bubble Wrap) is a lightweight, air-cellular protective packaging material that cushions fragile goods against impact, vibration, and abrasion during transport and storage. Available in small bubble (10mm), medium (20mm), and large bubble (30mm) formats in rolls and sheets. Used for wrapping electronics, glassware, ceramics, precision instruments, and furniture.
