Plywood Four-Way Pallet

Our Plywood Four-Way Pallets combine a smooth plywood deck with a 9-block hardwood base, enabling forklift and pallet jack access from all four sides. The block construction distributes load evenly and is preferred for automated warehouses, racking systems, and export cargo. The plywood surface ensures a clean, flat base ideal for pharmaceutical, food-grade, and FMCG goods.
