Wooden Crates

Our Wooden Crates are built for transporting and storing heavy, oversized, or irregularly shaped industrial goods — including machinery, engines, castings, moulds, and engineering equipment. Available in open-top and fully enclosed designs. Corner block reinforcement ensures stackability. The integrated pallet or skid base allows forklift handling. Custom internal brackets, foam dunnage, and pipe supports available.
