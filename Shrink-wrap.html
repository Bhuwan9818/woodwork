Shrink wrap

Our Shrink Wrap (Stretch Film) is an industrial-grade LLDPE stretch film used for unitising pallet loads, protecting goods from dust, moisture, and tampering during storage and transit. Available in hand roll and machine roll formats across multiple widths (500mm, 750mm) and gauges (17–25 micron). Pre-stretched film options reduce material usage by up to 50%. An essential packaging consumable supplied alongside our pallets for complete packaging solutions.
