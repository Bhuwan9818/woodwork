Our Story

From a Small Workshop to India's Trusted Pallet Partner

Founded in 2009, WoodCraft Pallets began as a small workshop in Delhi with a single mission: to manufacture wooden pallets that businesses could truly rely on. Today, we are one of India's leading manufacturers and suppliers of wooden pallets and packaging solutions.

Over 15 years, we've grown our production capacity, achieved ISPM-15 certification for export-grade pallets, and built a pan-India logistics network that serves 360+ clients across pharmaceuticals, automotive, FMCG, textiles, and heavy engineering.

Every pallet we make carries our commitment: quality you can measure, delivery you can count on.
